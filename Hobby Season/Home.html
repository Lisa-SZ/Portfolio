How to pick a hobby

In the warmer months such as Spring and Summer. Take advantage of the good weather and take your hobbies outdoors.

Outdoor Activities:

  • Photography
  • Walks
  • Gardening

In Autumn and Winter, you may find indoor activities more enjoyable instead of freezing outside.

Indoor Activities:

  • Crafts
  • Gaming
  • Reading

You can do any hobby whenever you want but spliting them up by season is a fun way to separate your interests!
